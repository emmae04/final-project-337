Blackjack rules

How to win:

The goal of the game is to get as close to 21 in card value as possible with the most amount of cards. You can choose as many cards as you want, but if you go over 21, you loose the game or go "bust".

You can play as many rounds as you wish. The winner of the game is the player that wins the majority of the rounds.

How the game works:

Both players will begin with 2 cards. You cannot see the other player's cards.

Both players then will take turns choosing whether or not to draw another card or to stick to their current cards. Each player will have 10 seconds to make their choice.

The turns continue until both players have stuck to their cards. Then, the player that is closest to 21 wins the round. If the cards are tied, the player with the most cards winds the round.

Aces count as 1. Jacks, Queens and Kings count as 10.
