Welcome to MANE games!

Here's how to navigate our site:

You have logged in, and on the previous page you'll be able to see information about your profile like who you you follow, who follows you, and your game stats. You can also edit your profile like changing your password.


If you hit the back button, you will be at the main page of the site. There you will see a collection of games you can play. To play the game, click the link next to the image of the game.

You can end any of the games whenever you want, and your scores will be saved to your profile so you can see them later.

Also in the main page you will find a button that leads you to the global leaderboard, and a button that leads to find friends on the site. To follow someone, click the follow button after you search them up in the search bar. To unfollow someone, click the unfollow button.

Have fun!
