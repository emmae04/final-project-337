Welcome to hangman! To play, enter your guess in the box.
Your guess must be either a single letter or the length of the word.
You will have 8 incorrect guesses until you lose the game.
To change difficulty, select from the menu your level.
Beginner will display a 5 letter word, Intermediate an 8 letter word, and Advanced will be a 12 letter word.
