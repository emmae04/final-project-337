Blackjack

A card game where player must try to get as close to 21 in card value as possible without going "bust" or over 21. You will play against a computer, and will receive points for winning rounds that will go towards your user score for the game. Click to play!

Boggle

Boggle is a word game in which the player tries to find as many words as they can from a grid of lettered dice, within a 3 minutes.

Hangman

This is a classic game of hangman! Pick between three levels: beginner, intermediate, and advanced. Enter your letter or word guesses to guess the word. But don't get more than 8 wrong answers, otherwise you lose!

Tic Tac Toe

A game in which you and an AI play in alternate turns to complete a row, a column, or a diagonal with either three O's or three X's drawn in the spaces of a grid of nine squares.
