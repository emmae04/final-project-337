hello and welcome to Tic Tac Toe! To play click any of the empty squares on the board, this will place an X spot for you . The robot will then make its move , an O.

To win , get your X's to be 3 in a row either, vertially horizontally, or diagonally. if the robot gets 3 O's in a row then the robot wins. if all pieces in the board are placed and neither you or the robot has won then it is a tie!

Point Breakdown:

Win: 5pts

Tie: 2pts

Lose: -2 pts
