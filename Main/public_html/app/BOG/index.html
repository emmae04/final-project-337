Boggle is a game where you must find as many words as possible in the "DiceTray". Once you hit the "Start Game" button you will have 3 minutes to find as many words in the diceTray as possible. You are able to connect letters in all directions but you may not repeat letters you have already used! The longer the words you find the more points you will receive.

Points (word Length -> points):
2 or below -> 0
3 -> 1
4 -> 1
5 -> 2
6 -> 3
7 -> 4
8 or more -> 11
